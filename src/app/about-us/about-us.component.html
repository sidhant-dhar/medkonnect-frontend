About Us

We are a group of individuals from different fields of work who have come together to help in thefight against the Novel Coronavirus Disease 2019 or COVID-19 pandemic.

At the frontlines of this fight are Our Doctors, Nurses and other medical staff who need to be equipped adequately with protective gear so as to avoid themselves from contracting this virus while risking themselves to treat others. In the face of this pandemic there is an alarming shortage of Personal Protective Equipment such as goggles, face-shield, mask and our goal is to match the rising demand of PPE with theirsupply.

Our main goal is to match the rise in demand for PPE to its supply to effectively combat COVID-19.

How we do it?

To achieve our goal we have created a platform that aggregates requests from hospitals and presents it before individuals or organizations that have a surplus of supply in a given area.

If you are a manufacturer of protective equipment, supplier, an NGO or anyone with additional equipment, now is the time to help our hospitals curb their short supply of PPE’s by registering on our website as a provider.

If you are a medical professional requiring PPE, reach out. We are trying to actively source PPE from medical suppliers to protect you, so that you can continue to protect us.

We also have designs and blueprints for the development of masks (N95/Surgical) and blueprints of other medical equipment on this website. If you are an individual or an SME looking to help by manufacturing and producing PPE and medical equipment, you can visit our site, download the materials and start right away.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to tobridge the gap between Demand and Supply. We do so by creating a platform whereSuppliers of Protective equipment are hosted alongside Medic Professionals whorequire them.

Our Solution

We have made provisions in our Solution for Medic Professionals to put forth their requirements and for Suppliers of PPE’s to have a clear view of the demand and meet the same.
