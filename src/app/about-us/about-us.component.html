Who are we?

We are a group of individuals from different fields of work who have come together to help in the fight against COVID-19.

At the frontlines of this fight are our Doctors, Nurses and other medical staff who need to be equipped adequately with protecive gear so as to avoid themselves from contracting this virus while risking themselves to treat others.

In the face of this pandemic there is an alarming shortage of Personal Protective Equipment such as goggles, face-shield, mask and our goal is to match the rising demand of PPE with their supply.

Our main goal is to match the rise in demand for PPE to its supply to effectively combat COVID-19

How we do it?

To achieve our goal we have created a platform that aggregates requests from hospitals and presents it before individuals or ogranistaions that have s surplus of supply in a given area.

If you are a manufacturer of Protective equipment, supplier, an NGO or anyone with additional equpiment, now is the time to help our hospitals curb their short supply of PPE's by registering on our website as a provider

If you are a medical professional requiring PPE, reach out, we are trying to actively source PPE from medical suppliers to protect you, so that you can continue to protect us

We also have designs and blueprints for the development of masks (N95/Surgical) and blueprints of other medical equipment on this website. If you are an individual or an SME looking to help by manufacturing and producing PPE and medical equipment, you can visit our site, download the materials and start right away.

Our Mission

Our mission to bridge the gap between demand and supply, we do so by creating a platform where suppliers of protective equipment are hosted alongside Medic Professionals who require them.

Our Solution

We have made provisions in our Solution for Medic Professionals to put forth their requirements and for suppliers of PPE's to have clear view of the demand and meet the same.
