HOW DOES MEDKONNECT WORK?

In the light of the COVID-19 pandemic, the supply of PPE to medical professionals has been severely disrupted, while the suppliers are unable to react to shortages in providingthese resources efficiently.

Our intelligent matching platform identifies and connects medical professionals facing critical shortages with suppliers who can supply them in the shortest possible time.
